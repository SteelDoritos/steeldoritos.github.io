About Me

SteelDoritos

Hello, my name is SteelDoritos and i am a civil engineer university student that started playing vrchat on 2020 because of the pandemic, since then i started to learn about unity, and later blender, substance and maybe even more.

After a few years of trial and error trying to learn different ways to cram a lot of things into avatar without it not being optimized, but also being completely functional, This Model was my main inspiration to do that, seeing a public avatar with a good ranking and lots of customization was really interesting and started my curiosity about how to optimize models

I feel there is always something new to learn about unity or blender and i am always up for learn, if i don´t know something, i will make sure to learn it for the next chance i got.
