Steelchi

Base model

Yeah, after a long time i finally got the guts to try and make a model from scratch (Yeah, modeling, rigging, mapping uvs and texturing)

First i was trying to make a tall nanachi but that main idea fell flat really fast, for a while i have been using a handmade mix of 2 base models for a while and i thought "Why shoulnt i try to make a whole base model with some of the best thing that i liked from those base models?" (Not using anything from those base models, only the general shape of my mix and a few things added that i think should be present in any base model besides some quality of life stuff)
​
The face went through multiple versions, just like the body, trying to find the best balance between appealing avatar and low poly one (The left one is the most recent one)

These were basically trial and error, since i really didnt knew what i was exactly going for (didnt knew what would be the sweet spot for it)
In this case i called v1.0 version of the avatar when it was able to do the bare minimun of any avi and had a basic shape
The idea with this avatar was to add in a "simple" base model everything i like about nanachi models while also trying to stay relatively low poly and really optimized on materials and at the same time looking appealing, and yeah, i also liked to make my own version of the coppouchi hands, since they are rare to find nowadays

- Polys: 18.416 tris (17.499 without hair)
- 2 materials for the body and 1 for the hair (I could have make it one, but people like to use eye shaders) 4k textures for the body and 2k for the eyes
- Toggleable nanachi and bunny tail (Everything in the same mesh besides the hair)
​- Good ranking avatar (Could be excelent if you remove a few more bones)
- Multiple facial expresions that can be combined 
- The hair is a separated mesh so you can easily replace it for your own in case you want to
- Hair: https://www.deviantart.com/celestcsilvari/art/MMD-PK-Ultra-Sun-and-Moon-Male-Hair-DL-803680014
​(The hair textures were upscaled and recolored)
Note: newer versions have a rework on the expressions and vicemes, so this images may be a bit outdated and missing some new expressions
