Steel new

Apparently one of the most used public avatars i have.

This avatar came out as a practice for myself with everything i learned of unity and blender at that point and try to add a lot of things in one avi without surpasing the "good" ranking from vrchat.
​
This avi ended up having up to 5 pieces of clothing that has animations for on/off and over 6 weapons that can be toggled and spin arround, also comes with a few secrets hand gestures combinations and also a working pen for quest and pc, i never acutally made the guns shot particles because of lack of time, but its completely doable to make it.

All pieces of clothing besides the harness are handmade and painted in blender. This avatar had over 32 materials and 15 meshes. I combined everything into a single mesh and texture atlas for Vrchat AND it also has less than 70k polys

My inspiration for making this was this avatar zealozoic.gumroad.com/l/kyominVRC , seeing a public avatar with a good ranking and lots of customisation was really interesting and started my curiosity about how to optimize models

I ended up making it public because i made my own model based on a coppouchi and a povichi, and i feelt like i would be glad to see more optimized avatars with quite a few toggles being arround
My first time modeling and texturing on blender from scratch (gun holster)
Also a few weapons i had in mind didnt ended up in the avatar
There are also another public avatars i made, but its easier to test them in vrchat rather than explain them here, and also some other avatars that will never see the light, like a completely working phone with contacts, and also a working murasama (metal gear rising) that works as you expect in the game.

But also there are a few projects i never finished and left incompleted that i doubt will ever see the light of day, they can come from like a fully functional Final Level of half life alyx with everything i really mean EVERYTHING, even the voices i will not spoil it but yeah, it was a really complex project to dumb silly memes.
